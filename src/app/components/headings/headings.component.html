HTML Headings
  1. Why we will use heading elements in HTML?

    2. The headings are used to define titles or sub titles that you want to display on web pages.

    HTML headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags. The <h1> defines the most imortant heading and the <h6>defines the least important heading.

    Browsers automatically adds the some margin before and after the heading.

h1 ----- is the most important heading.

h2 ----- is the second important heading.

h3 ----- is the third important heading.

h4 ----- is the fourth important heading.

h5 ----- is the fifth important heading.
h6 ----- is the least important heading.
